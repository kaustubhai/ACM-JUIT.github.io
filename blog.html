Vectors vs Stills

Why we need vectors?
Vectors are the most important part in a life of a Graphic Designer. We mainly have to deal with graphics that are vector and that’s too because it has some special characters which are lifesaving in many circumstances. A vector image has no bound at all for zooming in. It means that no matter how much you zoom into it or in other words no matter that at how much bigger screen you are going to display the vector, it’s still going to be pixel perfect without any disturbance. They are crisp, smooth and perfect all at once at any scale.

Augmented Reality

What is Augmented Reality (AR)?
Augmented Reality is a technology enriching the real world with digital information, media, or graphics overlaying in real time camera view of your smartphone or any other device. The word ‘Augment’ basically means to add or enhance something. So, in a layman manner, AR is when you look at the real world but its augmented with graphics in your view. The technology is providing itself as a very useful tool in our everyday life. From social media filters to surgical purposes to sports broadcasting, AR is rapidly growing popularity because it brings elements of virtual world into our real world, thus enhancing the things we see, hear and feel....

Q1. What is ACM ?
A1. The Association for Computing Machinery is a US-based international learned society for computing. It was founded in 1947, and is the world's largest scientific and educational computing society.

Q2. What does ACM do ?
A2. The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is the world's largest educational and scientific computing society, uniting educators, researchers and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources, and address the field's challenges.
