This ain't relay race but code

Everyone knows the relay race but here we are with something unique and a first time experience for the students of JUIT. An open to all event with a baton. A baton of mouse and a pen. It is played by team of 4 as the relay race. We tell the qquestion to th first member, he solves 1/4th problem, pass on the mouse and the pen to second member and he continues fior the next quarter untill the last person solves the problem. Difficult but interesting

Q1. What is ACM ?
A1. The Association for Computing Machinery is a US-based international learned society for computing. It was founded in 1947, and is the world's largest scientific and educational computing society.

Q2. What does ACM do ?
A2. The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is the world's largest educational and scientific computing society, uniting educators, researchers and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources, and address the field's challenges.
