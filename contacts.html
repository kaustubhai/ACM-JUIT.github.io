Q1. What is ACM ?
A1. The Association for Computing Machinery is a US-based international learned society for computing. It was founded in 1947, and is the world's largest scientific and educational computing society.

Q2. What does ACM do ?
A2. The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is the world's largest educational and scientific computing society, uniting educators, researchers and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources, and address the field's challenges.
